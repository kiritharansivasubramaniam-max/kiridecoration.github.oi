About Kiri Decoration

Kiri Decoration is a creative wedding décor company built on passion, culture, and the belief that every celebration deserves its own unique beauty. What started as a small dream has grown into a trusted name for elegant, thoughtful, and emotionally rich event styling.

“இரண்டு இதயங்கள் இணையும் தருணத்தை அழகாக மாற்றுவது எங்களின் பணி.” (“Making the moment where two hearts unite beautiful — that is our duty.”)

At Kiri Decoration, we understand that a wedding is more than an event — it is a moment of connection, tradition, and new beginnings. Our mission is to transform these moments into unforgettable visual experiences through décor that reflects love, personality, and cultural identity. We specialize in designing and styling everything from traditional Tamil weddings to modern luxury ceremonies. From floral mandaps, stage décor, and lighting design to reception themes, home events, and engagement setups, every element we create is crafted with precision and meaning. Our work blends timeless charm with contemporary aesthetics, delivering the perfect balance of tradition and elegance.

“தமிழ் பாரம்பரியத்தின் அழகை, நவீன வடிவத்தில் நாங்கள் பேசவைக்கிறோம்.” (“We let the beauty of Tamil tradition speak through modern design.”)

What sets Kiri Decoration apart is our deep respect for cultural heritage. Whether incorporating Sri Lankan Tamil elements, honoring traditional customs, or creating modern luxury themes, our team ensures every detail carries emotion and authenticity. Décor should not just look beautiful — it should feel personal, warm, and memorable. Every event we design is approached with care, creativity, and commitment. From the first consultation to the final setup, we work closely with each couple to understand their vision and bring it to life. Our aim is to make the planning process smooth and enjoyable while delivering décor that exceeds expectations. Over time, Kiri Decoration has become known for its artistic touch, unique concepts, and ability to transform ordinary spaces into breathtaking backdrops. Our designs have helped countless couples celebrate their love in settings that feel magical, intimate, and truly unforgettable. At Kiri Decoration, we don’t just decorate — we create stories, moments, and memories that stay in the heart forever.

Apart from wedding décor services in India, Kiri Decoration also provides worldwide export of wedding decoration materials for individuals and businesses who wish to start or expand their own event décor company. We proudly supply high-quality décor items to Europe, Australia, America, England, and many other countries, ensuring that our craftsmanship reaches clients across the globe. Whether you are launching a new wedding decoration business or looking to upgrade your existing inventory, we offer reliable, affordable, and beautifully crafted décor products tailored to your needs.

Our approach

Kiri Decoration follows a refined, design-driven approach.We study the venue, layout, color palette, and cultural elements to create a décor concept that blends luxury with elegance. Every stage of planning is executed with professionalism, from concept sketches to final installation, ensuring a seamless and sophisticated experience.

Meet the Founder

Kiritharan — The heart behind Kiri Decoration is Kiritharan, a proud Sri Lankan Tamil whose artistic roots lie in the cultural city of Jaffna. Once a profound and expressive artist, he brought his creativity and passion to Chennai, where he now designs beautiful wedding décor for couples who want something truly special. His work reflects the richness of Sri Lankan Tamil tradition, the warmth of his heritage, and the elegance of modern wedding aesthetics.
