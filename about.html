About Kiri Decoration

“A wedding is the sacred moment where two hearts become one — our purpose is to make that moment unforgettable.” At Kiri Decoration, we believe every celebration carries its own story, emotions, and beauty. At Kiri Decoration, we don’t just decorate spaces — we create moments that stay in your heart forever.
